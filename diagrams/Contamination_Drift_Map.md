# Contamination Drift Map

![Contamination Drift Map](Contamination_Drift_Map.png)

**Contamination Drift Map**

Shows how reflective instability rises when two interacting failure modes increase:

- **Overconfidence Mode Interaction:** Growing certainty applied to uncertain or low-signal inputs.
- **Drift Mode Oversight:** Weak or absent correction for accumulating small errors.

Higher regions (yellow/green) indicate rapid instability growth.  
Lower regions (blue/purple) reflect stable or well-regulated behavior.

This heatmap visualizes how combined oversight failures amplify reflective drift risk.
